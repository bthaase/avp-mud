Predator Skills
(Skill level 1 is considered 'Basic', Level 2 is 'Advanced', and Level 3 is 'Expert')
(It costs 500 exp for basic-level, 1000 exp for advanced and 2000 exp to reach expert level)

 (Green skills have been completed. Grey skills have not)

Note: Each skill has a 'round' counter that works similar to the attack guage. You may only use the skill when its guage is FULL. The rate at which a gauge replenishes is based on the 'Reset' field. 1 round is approximately one second. 

Detect Traps
Reset: 3 rounds
Level 2
(Skill Idea by Arigas)

 Description: Allows a scanning predator to spot hidden traps. 
Level 1: 40% chance to spot traps, up to one room away.
Level 2: 60% chance to spot traps, up to two rooms away. 
Level 3: 80% chance to spot traps, up to three rooms away.

Track
Reset: 4 rounds
Level 3

 Description: Allows you to TRACK targets by scent.
Level 1: Can TRACK up to 10 rooms away.
Level 2: Can TRACK up to 15 rooms away.
Level 3: Can TRACK up to 20 rooms away.

Vision Modes
Reset: N/A
Level 4

 Description: Allows you to SWITCH vision modes. (Gives all modes)
Level 1: Can scan up to two rooms in a vision mode.
Level 2: Can scan up to three rooms in a vision mode.
Level 3: Can scan up to four rooms in a vision mode.

Nightvision
Reset: N/A
Level 5

 Description: Passive Skill. Allows you to see in the dark, but with a penalty. 
Level 1: Your perception is -10 in dark rooms.
Level 2: Your perception is -5 in dark rooms.
Level 3: No penalty to perception.

Shoulder Cannon
Reset: 8 rounds
Level 6

 Description: Drains charge. Use SC (direction) (target) to fire. Very accurate.
Level 1: Max range of 1 room. Deals 60 energy damage.
Level 2: Max range of 2 rooms. Deals 80 energy damage.
Level 3: Max range of 3 rooms. Deals 100 energy damage.

Medical
Reset: N/A
Level 7

 Description: Allows you access to use medical items.
Level 1: Allows you to USE the Sterilizing daggers.
Level 2: Allows you to USE the Medikits.
Level 3: Allows you to USE the Medicomp to heal. (Costs charge)

Wrist Rocket
Reset: 60 rounds
Level 8

Description: Fires a one-shot rocket from your wrist gauntlet in a direction.
Level 1: Deals 50 fire damage, Burns for 3 rounds. 
Level 2: Deals 75 fire damage, Burns for 4 rounds.
Level 3: Deals 100 fire damage, Burns for 5 rounds.

Tumble
Reset: 25 rounds
Level 9

 Description: Passive - Automatically dodges an explosion that might hit you.
Level 1: 50% chance when Tumble is charged.
Level 2: 75% chance when Tumble is charged.
Level 3: 100% chance when Tumble is charged.

Trap Setting
Reset: 10 rounds
Level 10

 Description: Allows you to SET traps to snag clumsy foe. 
Level 1: 15 rounds to place a trap. 60% chance of success.
Level 2: 10 rounds to place a trap. 80% chance of success.
Level 3: 5 rounds to place a trap. 100% chance of success.

Double Strike
Reset: 30 rounds
Level 11

 Description: Gives you a chance of doing two attacks in a row. (Melee weapons)
Level 1: 50% chance. Must be fully charged.
Level 2: 75% chance. Must be fully charged.
Level 3: 100% chance. Must be fully charged.

Chainstrike
Reset: 30 rounds
Level 12

 Description: Gives you a chance of hitting all enemies in the room. (Melee only)
Level 1: 50% Chance. Accuracy decreases by 15% per additional target.
Level 2: 70% Chance. Accuracy decreases by 10% per additional target.
Level 3: 90% Chance. Accuracy decreases by 5% per additional target.

Valor
Reset: N/A
Level 13

 Description: Passive - Takes effect once health drops below 20%.
Level 1: Increases damage to 110%, and accuracy to 110%.
Level 2: Increases damage to 130%, and accuracy to 120%.
Level 3: Increases damage to 150%, and accuracy to 130%. 

Parry
Reset: 20 rounds
Level 14

 Description: Deflects all ranged attacks. Drains 1 AP/MP per blocked attack.
Level 1: Parry lasts up to 2 ticks or until another action is taken.
Level 2: Parry lasts up to 3 ticks or until another action is taken.
Level 3: Parry lasts up to 4 ticks or until another action is taken.

Alien Affinity
Reset: N/A
Level 15

 Description: Provides the Predator with Alien-related bonuses. Passive.
Level 1: 20% harder to disarm. Immune to Acid spray.
Level 2: Accuracy vs. Alien goes up by 20%
Level 3: Danger Sense - Detects hidden aliens when entering a room.

Primal Instinct
Reset: 30 rounds
Level 16

 Description: While charged, functions as a one-shot dodge, then it must recharge.
Level 1: 25% chance to dodge an attack that would have landed.
Level 2: 50% chance to dodge an attack that would have landed.
Level 3: 75% chance to dodge an attack that would have landed.

Sixth Sense
Reset: 1 round
Level 17

 Description: Alerts you when a enemy moves within your sense range.
Level 1: Can detect enemies up to one rooms away.
Level 2: Can detect enemies up to two rooms away.
Level 3: Can detect enemies up to three rooms away.

Battlecry
Reset: 20 rounds
Level 18

 Description: All targets within the radius must make 'Scare' checks.
Level 1: Radius of one room, but heard up to three away.
Level 2: Radius of two rooms, but heard up to five away.
Level 3: Radius of three rooms, but heard up to seven away.

Human Technology
Reset: N/A

 Description: Allows the player to use various human devices.
Level 1: Allows you to use marine items. (Motion detector, GPS, etc)
Level 2: Allows you to use marine weapons. (50% accuracy penalty)
Level 3: Allows you to use marine vehicles.
