Attack Command Summery



Generic Attack Commands:

Syntax Listing

Attack Description and Information

KILL
(All races)
[Progress: 100%]

 Syntax: Kill (target)
Generic, point-blank combat skill. Usable by all races, although its recommend the Marines and Predators stick to ranged attacks when at all possible. KILL will attack a single target in your current room, with your current weapon.

LOB
(Marine, Predator) 
[Progress: 100%]

 Syntax: Lob (direction) (rooms)
Generic explosives skill. Lobs the currently held item in the specified direction, up to (str / 5) rooms away. Allows you to very precisely hit target locations.

THROW
(Marine, Predator) 
[Progress: 50%]

 Syntax: Throw (direction) (target)
Very similar to LOB, except that its being thrown at a specific target in the specified direction. Normal objects deal (weight * 2) damage when thrown, although certain items are handled differently. If a melee weapon hits a target, it lands one standard attack, as if the thrower had walked in and used KILL. 

SNIPE
(Marine, Predator) 
[Progress: 100%]

 Syntax: Snipe (direction) (target)
Common attack command used by Marines and Predators. Fires against a single target up to the weapons max range in rooms away. Unlike KILL, the more rounds per shot, the worse the accuracy. Single-Fire or Semi-automatic mode is best.

SMASH
(Alien, Predator) 
[Progress: 100%]

 Syntax: Smash (object)
Not much of an attack, although good for preventing them. Attempts to 'smash' a target object in the current room. This can include other species weapons, and most importantly, Sentry turrets. You don't want those things just sitting around. 

Marine Attack Commands:

Syntax Listing

Attack Description and Information

SPRAY
(Progress: 100%)

 Syntax: Spray (direction)
The marines most common ranged attack command. Your weapons must be set to either Burst fire or full-automatic. Fires up to the max range in the specified direction, hitting targets at random, including other Marines. Use with caution.

BLAST
(Progress: 0%)

 Syntax: Blast (direction)
Only works with shotguns. Similar to spray, except it can only hit one target at random, dealing between 50-75% of its normal damage. Blast will travel up to its max range, but hits the closest targets first. Most shotguns are short range anyhow.

FIRE
(Progress: 100%)

 Syntax: Fire (direction) [optional range]
Designed for rocket and grenade launchers, and weapon attachments if mounted on the current gun. Fires a round up to the specified range (or weapons max range if over), in the target direction. If no range is specified, travels up to max range.

Alien Attack Commands:

Syntax Listing

Attack Description and Information

TACKLE
(Progress: 100%)

 Syntax: Tackle (target)
Very useful alien attack. Resets the AP Gauge, but stuns the target for ( str / 10 ), or 1-3  rounds. More importantly, if your Str+5 is greater than the targets strength, they get disarmed and drop their current weapon(s) to the ground.

Predator Attack Commands:

Syntax Listing

Attack Description and Information

AIM
(Progress: 0%)

 Syntax: Aim (body location)
Allows the Predator to aim for specific body locations when attacking. Lowers attack accuracy by 20%, but its well worth it. Below is a list of locations and effect:
Head : Damage x 2
Arm  : Lowers attack accuracy
Hand : Drops held/wielded/dual-wield item at random.
Leg  : Slows movement rate
