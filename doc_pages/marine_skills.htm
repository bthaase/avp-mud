Marine Skills
(Skill level 1 is considered 'Basic', Level 2 is 'Advanced', and Level 3 is 'Expert')
(It costs 500 exp for basic-level, 1000 exp for advanced and 2000 exp to reach expert level)

 (Red skills have been completed. Grey skills have not)

Note: Each skill has a 'round' counter that works similar to the attack gauge. You may only use the skill when its gauge is FULL. The rate at which a gauge replenishes is based on the 'Reset' field. 1 round is approximately one second. 

Light Armor Proficiency
Reset: N/A
Level 2

 Description: Affords the player better protection when using Light Armor.
Level 1: Decreases armor damage by 20%
Level 2: Decreases armor damage by 40%
Level 3: Decreases armor damage by 60%

Hand Weapons
Reset: N/A
Level 3

 Description: Increases your skill with handguns and pistols.
Level 1: Increases your accuracy by 10%, and damage by 10%
Level 2: Increases your accuracy by 20%, and damage by 20%
Level 3: Increases your accuracy by 30%, and damage by 30%

Basic Medical
Reset: 4 rounds
Level 4

 Description: Allows the player to TREAT another marines wounds.
Level 1: Requires bandages. HP + 10. Won't heal over 70%
Level 2: Requires bandages. HP + 20. Won't heal over 80%
Level 3: Requires bandages. HP + 30. Wont heal over 90%

Level 3: Increases SCAN range by 3 rooms.

Backup Pistol
Reset: 6 rounds
Level 6

 Description: Passive Skill. Triggers when your AP is empty during a KILL.

Radio Contact
Reset: 15 Rounds
Level 9
