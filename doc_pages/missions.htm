Automatic Missions:
 Automatic missions are always available, and can be done at any time for experience.

 Marines: 
  Mission: Find survivors
  Description: Find living human civilians and soldiers, and you'll get an exp bonus.
 Predator:
  Mission: Collect trophies
  Description: Simply collect the skulls of your prey, and you'll get an experience bonus.
 Xenomorph:
  Mission: Capture hosts
  Description: Use 'grab' to carry hosts back to the hive, and you'll get an exp bonus.


Missions Listing:
  Mission: Predator Vessel
  Description: A Hunter Ship is being held in a USCM Research facility.
  Time Limit: 30 minutes
  Objectives:
   [Marine]
    (Primary) Defend the facility from the Predators.
   [Predator]
    (Primary) Free the predator ship from the human facility.
   [Xenomorph]
    (Primary) Attempt to disable or destroy the predator vessel.

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Mission: Sabotage Human Experiments
  Description: Humans have obtained valuable alien or predator artifacts, and
   are attempting to unlock their secrets.
  Time Limit: 20 minutes
  Objectives:
   [Marine]
    (Primary) Protect the alien/predator artifacts.
    (Secondary) Protect the central computer core and its data.
   [Predator]
    (Primary) Steal back or destroy the stolen artifacts
   [Xenomorph]
    (Primary) Destroy the central computer core, and with it the research.

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Mission: Evacuation
  Description: A group of survivors have been spotted near the base, and are
   under heavy attack by aliens and hunters. Any left after the time runs
   out is an extra 100 XP to the Predators. (Roughly 25 survivors)
  Time Limit: 30 minutes
  Objectives:
   [Marine]
    (Primary) Return as many survivors to the base as possibly. (+200 Xp each)
   [Predator]
    (Primary) Prevent the rescue of the survivors by killing their saviors.
   [Xenomorph]
    (Primary) Turn as many survivors as possible, and return them to the hive. (+300 Xp each)
