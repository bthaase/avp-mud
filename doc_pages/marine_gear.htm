Marine Weapons:

Name Type Cost Details Comments

Standard-Issue Flashlight

 Light 25 XP

Provides Eight (8) Hours of Light for a marine. Can be turned on and off with USE. 

Always have one of these. In fact, keep a spare in your pack as well. You will be crying if you find yourself in deep territory on LV-426 in the dark with no light. Just remember to turn them off when it's morning again.

USCM Field Radio

 Radio 100  XP

Allows Marines to make use of their local-area RADIO Command, for communicating with each other. 

Incredibly useful to have. Always make sure you have one of these in your inventory, so you can stay in contact with your fellow marines.

USCM Binoculars

 Binoculars 250 XP

Provides +5 Perception while worn. 

Not incredibly useful, right now they provide +5 to Perception, which can be useful if you can't afford to buy the attributes yet. Also, they'll soak a few hits of  'Acid Spit'

USCM GPS Device

 GPS 300  XP

Reports your current X/Y/Z Coordinates when used. Useful for Airstrike and related commands. 

A spendy gizmo for a few digits, but most of the new arenas focus around '0 0 0' being the marine objective - So it can be useful for finding your way around sometimes.

USCM  Motion Tracker

 Motion Tracker 500 XP

While held, indicates any motion within its 30 meter range..

A spammy device, but a tool that will save your life in numerous situations. Keep in mind you will not be able to use this with heavy weapons.
