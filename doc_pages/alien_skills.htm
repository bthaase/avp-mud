Alien Skills
(Skill level 1 is considered 'Basic', Level 2 is 'Advanced', and Level 3 is 'Expert')
(It costs 500 exp for basic-level, 1000 exp for advanced and 2000 exp to reach expert level)

 (Blue skills have been completed. Grey skills have not)

Note: Each skill has a 'round' counter that works similar to the attack guage. You may only use the skill when its guage is FULL. The rate at which a gauge replenishes is based on the 'Reset' field. 1 round is approximately one second. 

Dodge
Reset: 6 rounds
Level 2

 Description: Allows you to dodge incoming ranged attacks such as SPRAY.
Level 1: 10% chance to dodge incoming attacks. 
Level 2: 20% chance to dodge incoming attacks.
Level 3: 30% chance to dodge incoming attacks.

Evasive
Reset: N/A
Level 3

 Description: Makes it harder for enemies to hit you.
Level 1: 10% penalty to enemy accuracy checks.
Level 2: 20% penalty to enemy accuracy checks.
Level 3: 30% penalty to enemy accuracy checks.

Tail Slam
Reset: 8 rounds
Level 4

 Description: Can SLAM a target, injuring them. Short usage delay.
Level 1: Deals light damage (Strength + 10, impact). Stuns for 2-6 rounds.
Level 2: Deals light damage (Strength + 20, impact). Stuns for 6-10 rounds.
Level 3: Deals medium damage (Strength + 30, impact). Stuns for 10-14 rounds.

Battle Method
Reset: 12 rounds
Level 5

 Description: When triggered, your attack will bypass armor. Only when reset.
Level 1: 10% chance of triggering on attack.
Level 2: 20% chance of triggering on attack.
Level 3: 30% chance of triggering on attack.

Alien Rage
Reset: 90 rounds
Level 6

 Description: While active, you automatically absorb 50% of all damage you take.
Level 1: Alien Rage lasts 10 rounds or until you die.
Level 2: Alien Rage lasts 15 rounds or until you die.
Level 3: Alien Rage lasts 20 rounds or until you die.

Critical Strike
Reset: 12 rounds
Level 7

 Description: Critical strikes deal double damage. Only works when 'reset'
Level 1: 10% chance of landing a critical strike.
Level 2: 20% chance of landing a critical strike.
Level 3: 30% chance of landing a critical strike.

Acid Spit
Reset: 12 rounds
Level 8

 Description: Allows you to SPIT at a target. Costs health.
Level 1: Blinds target for 10-20 rounds. Costs 4 Hp.
Level 2: Blinds target for 20-30 rounds. Costs 6 Hp.
Level 3: Blinds target for 30-40 rounds. Costs 8 Hp.

Leap
Reset: 8 rounds
Level 9

 Description: Launches yourself in a direction. Costs movement.
Level 1: Leap up to two rooms in a direction.
Level 2: Leap up to four rooms in a direction.
Level 3: Leap up to four rooms. And through doors.

Breach
Reset: 12 rounds
Level 10

 Description: Allows aliens to chew, claw, and tear their way into certain areas.
Level 1: Can BREACH closed doors. (Shreds 'em)
Level 2: Can BREACH armored doors. 
Level 3: Can BREACH into vehicle cockpits.

Tail Sting
Reset: 60 rounds
Level 11

 Description: Stings a target and injects a paralytic venom. 
Level 1: 50% chance of hitting. Stuns target for 15 rounds.
Level 2: 70% chance of hitting. Stuns target for 30 rounds.
Level 3: 90% chance of hitting. Stuns target for 45 rounds.

Lunge
Reset: 8 rounds
Level 12

 Description: Flying leap attack. Costs movement and resets Attack guage.
Level 1: Two rooms. Hits a single target. Stuns for 5-10 rounds.
Level 2: Three rooms. Deals 50% more damage, and stuns for 10-15 rounds.
Level 3: Three rooms, and drops multiple targets if none specified. Same stun.

Acid Potency
Reset: N/A
Level 13

 Description: Increases the damage dealt from your acid splash.
Level 1: Acid damage is increased by 10%
Level 2: Acid damage is increased by 20%
Level 3: Acid damage is increased by 30%

Head Bite
Reset: 12 rounds
Level 14

 Description: Devastating attack. Replenishes your health. (Uses BITE)
Level 1: Only targets under 40% Hp. Replenishes 20% of your health.
Level 2: Only targets under 60% Hp. Replenishes 35% of your health.
Level 3: Only targets under 80% Hp. Replenishes 50% of your health.

Confuse
Reset: 60 rounds
Level 15

 Description: Aliens rush around, blinding motion detectors and scaring marines.
Level 1: Lasts 10 rounds. Affects up to 30 meters away.
Level 2: Lasts 15 rounds. Affects up to 40 meters away.
Level 3: Lasts 20 rounds. Affects up to 50 meters away.

Acute Hearing
Reset: N/A
Level 16

 Description: Allows the alien to hear 'sounds' and enemy actions. (IE: Reloading)
Level 1: Can hear up to two rooms away.
Level 2: Can hear up to three rooms away.
Level 3: Can hear up to four rooms away.

Pursuit
Reset: 6 rounds
Level 17

 Description: Allows an alien to TRACK  its prey by scent.
Level 1: Can TRACK up to 10 rooms away.
Level 2: Can TRACK up to 20 rooms away.
Level 3: Can TRACK up to 30 rooms away.

Pheromones
Reset: 60 rounds
Level 18

 Description: Allows the alien to call in support from nearby aliens via SCENT.
Level 1: Draws aliens from up to four rooms away.
Level 2: Draws aliens from up to six rooms away.
Level 3: Draws aliens from up to eight rooms away.

Hive Construction
 Reset: N/A
Level 19

 Description: Passive. Increases your building skills with Hive and Construct.
Level 1: HIVE Command takes 1/2 time to complete.
Level 2: CONSTRUCT takes 1/2 time to complete.
Level 3: You have 50% more Resin for use with Hive/Construct.

Lay Eggs
Reset: 120 rounds
Level 20

 Description: Allows the character to lay eggs in hived areas.
Level 1: Lay one weak egg. Costs 100% of your movement.
Level 2: Lay one medium egg. Costs 90% of your movement.
Level 3: Lay one tough egg. Costs 80% of your movement.
